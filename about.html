About Enhlosweni

Rooted in Purpose, Driven by Innovation

Who We Are

Enhlosweni is a youth-led enterprise founded by Lungile Anele Mbatha. Our mission is to empower students by bridging the gap between education and real-world experience. We provide opportunities for first-year university students to work on live projects, gain credibility, and build their portfolios.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable ecosystem where youth can discover, develop, and showcase their talents while contributing to the digital economy and reducing unemployment.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative tech solutions while creating opportunities for young talent to gain hands-on experience, credibility, and confidence in their chosen fields.

Our Core Values

Communication

We believe in open, respectful, and transparent teamwork.

Dedication

We give our best in everything we do to achieve excellence.

Innovation

We create solutions that are impactful and future-ready.

Accountability

We own our responsibilities and deliver on our commitments.
